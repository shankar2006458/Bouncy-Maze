📖 How to Play
🔮Guide the Orb: Swipe or use arrow keys to bounce the energy orb. It slides until it hits a wall.
💡Light Tiles: Every tile the orb bounces over becomes energized. Light up ALL path tiles to win!
◇Teleporters: Purple tiles warp you to their matching pair. Use them strategically!
➤One-Way Paths: Orange tiles only allow movement in one direction. Plan carefully!
⭐Stars: Earn up to 3 stars per level based on move efficiency.
